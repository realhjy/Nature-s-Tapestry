我们收集了部分鸟类叫声，并利用数字音频处理技术对这段音频进行分析，生成了两种不同类型的语谱图，以更好地理解鸟类的声音特征。

第一幅是帧大小为64、帧覆盖率为50%的宽带语谱图，它能够呈现出鸟类叫声的整体频谱特征，帮助我们研究它们的声音模式和频率分布。

第二幅是帧大小为4096、帧覆盖率为50%的窄带语谱图，它能够提供更高分辨率的频谱信息，使我们能够更准确地观察鸟类叫声中的细微变化和频率细节。

我们希望借助数字音频处理技术，生成并分析语谱图，为人们提供一个更全面、立体的鸟类声音世界，唤起人们对自然环境和生物多样性的保护意识，为保护和维护生物多样性做出贡献，促进人与自然之间的和谐共生。

We have collected a portion of bird vocalizations and utilized digital audio processing techniques to analyze the audio, generating two different types of spectrograms to better understand the sound characteristics of birds.

The first spectrogram is a wideband spectrogram with a frame size of 64 and a frame overlap rate of 50%. It showcases the overall spectral features of bird vocalizations, aiding us in studying their sound patterns and frequency distribution.

The second spectrogram is a narrowband spectrogram with a frame size of 4096 and a frame overlap rate of 50%. It provides higher resolution spectral information, allowing us to observe subtle variations and frequency details within bird vocalizations more accurately.

By leveraging digital audio processing techniques to generate and analyze spectrograms, we aim to provide a comprehensive and immersive world of bird sounds. Our goal is to raise awareness about the importance of protecting the natural environment and biodiversity. Through these efforts, we strive to contribute to the preservation and maintenance of biodiversity, fostering a harmonious coexistence between humans and nature.
